I'm an AI software engineer currently based in Paris. I graduated from EHTP (Ecole Hassania of Public Works). Prior to EHTP, I studied Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Computer Science in CPGE.

My primary interests are data science, machine learning and deep learning. I'm especially fascinated by the intersection of sentiment analysis and AI, and by the ways AI can reshape healthcare and other real-world domains. I enjoy building projects, translating research into reproducible tools, and collaborating with interdisciplinary teams.

In my free time I love working on projects, creating content, writing poetry, practicing yoga, and photography. I also enjoy having interesting conversations with curious, like-minded people and I'm always open to collaboration.
